Rules

  • Snake Must Eat The Red Apples to Grow Longer.
  • Snake Must Not Hit The Border Walls.
  • Snake Must Not Bite Its Own Body.
  • Snake Can't Travel in Reverse Without Turning Around.

Scoring

  • Snake Gets 10 Points for Each Apple Eaten.
  • If Final Total Points is Less Than 150, You Get 1 Star Rating.
  • If Final Total Points is 150+ But Less than 300, You Get 2 Stars Rating.
  • If Final Total Points is 300 or More, You Get 3 Stars Rating.

Keyboard Keys To Use

USAGE
